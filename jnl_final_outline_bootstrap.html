Rapekit Backlog Leads to Backlog in Procecution

An estimated hundreds of thousands of rapekits remain untested in police and crime labs across the U.S. The lack of policies, protocal and resouces for rape kit testing have caused a backlog in timely and crutial evidence. This prevents survivors from recieving justice and allows offenders to go unprosecuted, thus tolerating rape and rape culture in the U.S.
